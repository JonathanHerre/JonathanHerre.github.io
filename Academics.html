Academics achievements

Middle School Valedictorian

One of the academic achievements that I have made during these two years and that I am proud of is being the valedictorian of my middle school. This achievement is important for me because I had to give a speech in front of my classmates and parents when I was still struggling with pronunciation. I was given this recognition thanks to my grades and STAR 360 results.

Get Accepted Into Lane

Although many people might not consider this an academic achievement, I personally think it is. This is because, after only six months of living here, I had to take the high school entrance exam while thinking how to adapt to an entirely different educational system at a selective high school that receives more than 10,000 applications every year. I had to maintain all A's in my grades and pass the algebra test because I really wanted to get in.

Jonathan Herrera | Lane Tech 9th Grader
