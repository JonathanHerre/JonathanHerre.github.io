Projects I've done during this school year

One project I completed this year that I am proud of is my Service Learning Project for English, where I highlighted an issue common in many cities: their lack of walkability. This project is important not only because it addresses an important urban concern but also because completing a Service Learning project is a mandatory graduation requirement.

Another project I have completed this year, which I am particularly proud of, is my Service Learning Project for Science. In this project, I addressed a significant issue in the city of Chicago: the impact of long commutes to work on the likelihood of obesity and how it affects the prevalence of Type 2 diabetes. This project is also very important, as it is a mandatory requirement for graduation. It also includes a letter that was sent to Mayor Brandon Thompson, urging him to address this issue.
